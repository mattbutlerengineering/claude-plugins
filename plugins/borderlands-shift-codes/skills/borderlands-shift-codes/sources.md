## Code Verification Tips

1. **Check the date**: Ensure the article was updated recently
2. **Cross-reference**: Verify codes appear on multiple sources
3. **Look for expiration info**: Many sites list when codes expire
4. **Test quickly**: Codes can expire without warning
5. **Track your codes**: Use the tracking feature to avoid duplicates

- **Region**: Most codes work globally, but verify if issues occur
- **Rate limits**: Don't redeem too many codes too quickly on the website
